The World Athletics Continental Tour is a global series of track and field meetings that sits directly below the elite Diamond League in prestige. Events are ranked into five tiers — Diamond (highest, invitation-only) → Gold → Silver → Bronze → Challenger (lowest) — reflecting prize money, athlete quality, and organisational standards, so the tier tells you at a glance how competitive a meeting is likely to be.
